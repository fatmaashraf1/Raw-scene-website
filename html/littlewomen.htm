Little Women

A simple story about four young girls' sisters coming to womanhood,
their preaching father goes with the Union Army during the Civil War,
the girls stay with their mother and they light house fires daily,
Meg is the firstborn, and is a second mother to the rest of her sisters,
while Josephine is The girl who takes on the man of the house,
Beth is the most submissive girl,
and the youngest of the sisters is Amy, who strives for the good in life.
